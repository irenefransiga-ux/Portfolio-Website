About

My Story

Hi there, my name is Irene Fransiga and I am a CareerFoundry certified UX Designer with a computer science degree and professional experience in the hospitality industry.

Having completed over 600+ hours of User Experience Intensive Course, I have developed the ability to approach a problem analytically and the ability to look at designs and products from the user’s point of view. Through CareerFoundry, I have also developed User Interface Design skills such as understanding Gestalt Principles and Principles of Design.

Before I decided that UX Designer was for me, I had completed my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science which had equipped me with valuable skills transitioning to be a UX Designer such as front-end programming languages, organizing information in an understandable manner, collaboration skills, problem-solving skills, as well as being able to communicate effectively with developers.

As a UX Designer, I believe that our works have a greater impact on everyday life. Just like what Steve Jobs said “Design is not just how it looks and feels like. Design is how it works.” I apply user-centered design in all my works and my goal is to understand what you want and what you need. To achieve my goal, I spent some time analyzing collected research data and use them as a means of understanding your user’s needs.
